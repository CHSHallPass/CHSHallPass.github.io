Welcome to The CHS Hall Pass website.

Our mission is to provide teachers with information about students' time spent in class versus in the hallway in an attempt to raise the level of class participation. We aim to provide an online resource for teachers, students, and administration to better monitor and regulate the amount of time spent out of class.

We believe that everyone has the potential to improve in class which can be directly related to how much time is spent in class. With The CHS Hall Pass Website, we can more easily help people who spend more time outside of class. We welcome you to join us on this journey of improvement by using and respecting The CHS Hall Pass website.
